Ingredients and Amount

  • 4 Tbsp Ching's Schezwan Chutney.
  • 2 Tbsp Oill.
  • 1 TbspMustard Seeds.
  • 8 to 10 Curry Leaves.
  • 2 Chopped Green Chilli.
  • 1 Chopped Onion.

Instrucations :

  1. Blanch the poha in water and then soak it.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, green chilli, and onion. Saute for 2 mins
  3. Add Poha and Ching's Secret Schezwan Chutney. Cook for 2-3 mins
  4. Garnish with chopped coriander leaves
